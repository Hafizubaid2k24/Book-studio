The Silent Invasion

★★★★☆ (4.7/5)

Maqbool Ahmad ki likhi hui ek behtareen Islamic suspense novel jo sachi kahaniyan aur ruhani aqdaam se bhari hai.

Musannif (Author): Maqbool Ahmad

Safhaat (Pages): 300

Qeemat (Price): $18.00

Level: Tamam levels

Kitabon par wapas jayen

Kitab ki Tafseel

The Silent Invasion by Maqbool Ahmad ek misali Islamic suspense fiction hai jo dilchasp kahaniyan aur gehri soch dono pesh karti hai. Iska mystery, action, aur ruhaniyat ka milap usay ek dilchasp qira'at banata hai. Yeh kitab hamesha hamein yaad dilati hai ke iman, jurrat, aur ittifaq hamari dunya mein sukoon aur insaf ko tabah karne wali khamosh quwaton ke khilaf zaroori hathyaar hain.

Kitab ki Tafseel
